Founder

Led by experience. Built on clarity.

Remember that kid in school who was overly organised for no reason? Yeah, that was me. Fast forward through 20+ years, an MBA from a Top-100 US B-School, and building marketing functions across four continents — I've turned that obsession into something I'm genuinely proud of.

I specialise in GTM strategies that don't just sit in fancy PowerPoints. I've scaled businesses, architected entire marketing functions from the ground up, and generated measurable growth across industries — from cybersecurity to streaming, from startups to global enterprises.

From solopreneur to leading teams across the US, Australia, and Dubai, I've navigated enough business challenges to know one thing for certain: strategy only works when it's built to execute.

Beyond boardrooms and brand decks, I've trained students and professionals at some of India's most respected institutions — IIT, IIM, Emeritus, and Symbiosis, among others. My work in this space has always been about one thing: closing the gap between what academia teaches and what the real world demands.

And when I finally drag myself out of the marketing trenches? I'm a photographer, a fitness enthusiast, and a hardcore animal lover. Whether I'm mentoring professionals or running miles before sunrise, I believe in pushing limits — just like I do in everything else.
